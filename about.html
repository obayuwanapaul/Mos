About Us

MOS Energy limited is an indigenous Company principally owned and operated by Nigerians. The Company was incorporated in March, 2007 under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. It commenced operation in January, 2009. The corporate office is located at The Oceanview Place, No. 2, Soji Olagunju Street, Alpha Beach Road, Lekki Peninsula, Lagos – Nigeria.

MOS Energy Company Limited, an Oil and Gas energy company, is in partnership with Platform Petroleum Limited / Newcross Petroleum Limited Joint Venture and is a member of the Board of Directors of PNG Gas Limited.

MOS Energy Company Limited (MOS Energy) was formed to own, operate, acquire and develop a diversified portfolio of natural gasand complementary midstream energy assets. Our primary objective is to provide end-to-end gas solutionsto oil and gas producers i.e. bring Gas and Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs) from the well head to the End Users.

MOS Energy Company Limited provides solutions that reduce / eliminate gas flaring, maximize local content and contribute to the development of the domestic LPG and Propane markets in Nigeria. In this regard, the Company is involved in the haulage and marketing of Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) and Propane. It is also involved in the marketing of Condensate and Lean Gas.

The primary objectiveof MOS Energy istoprovideend-to-end gas solutions to oil and gas producers. In simple term, we bring gas and Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs) from the well head to the end user. In addition, we provide solutions that reduce and eliminate gas flaring,maximize local content and contribute to the development of the domestic LPG market in Nigeria.

In achieving this,we gather,process,transport and market Natural Gas and Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs). We have developed assets which position us to become a leading natural gas processor,bulk distributor of LPG (cookinggas) and Propane and transporter of CNG (Compressed Natural Gas). In conjunction with Platform Petroleum Ltd andother International Partners, we plan to acquire additional assets and have a management team dedicated to executing ours long- term strategies.
